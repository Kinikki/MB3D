About MB3D Creations

Hi! We’re MB3D Creations, a family-run 3D printing shop started by a mom and child who love creating colorful, fun, and useful things together.

What started as a hobby quickly became something we wanted to share with others. Every creation is printed layer by layer with care, patience, and plenty of excitement.

We make items like articulated animals, keychains, home organizers, gifts, decorations, and other fun 3D printed creations.

Some items are printed from licensed designs created by talented artists, and some items may be personalized or customized depending on the product.

Creator Credits & Licensing

MB3D Creations respects the work of 3D model designers and digital artists. Some of the physical products we sell are printed from digital files created by independent designers.

When a commercial license is required, we purchase and follow the licensing terms for that creator. MB3D Creations sells the physical printed item and does not claim ownership of digital models created by other artists.
