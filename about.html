Made by Mommy & Bean • Printed Layer by Layer

💜 Our Story

MB3D Creations is a family-run small business created by a mom and kid who love making people smile. What started as a hobby quickly became a passion for creating colorful 3D printed creations, from adorable articulated creatures to practical organizers and unique gifts. Every item is carefully printed, inspected, and packaged with the same care we'd want if we were receiving it ourselves.

🌈 What We Make

  • Articulated creatures
  • Keychains
  • Fidget toys
  • Desk accessories
  • Home organization
  • Holiday decorations
  • Custom gifts
  • Personalized creations

🖨️ Our Promise

Every print is made to order. We believe quality matters more than speed, so each creation is checked before it ships. If a print isn't something we'd proudly keep ourselves, it doesn't leave our printer.

🎨 Creator Credits & Licensing

Many of the physical products we sell are printed from digital designs created by talented independent artists. Whenever required, MB3D Creations purchases commercial licenses and follows each designer's licensing terms. We proudly support the creators who make these amazing models possible.

MB3D Creations manufactures and sells the physical printed products only. All copyrights remain with their respective designers.
