❓Frequently Asked Questions

Most items are printed and shipped within a few business days, depending on size and order volume.

Can I request different colors?

Absolutely! Most products can be printed in different filament colors.

Do you design every model?

Some products are original creations, while others are printed from licensed models created by talented independent designers. Whenever required, we purchase commercial licenses and provide creator credit.
